Get to Know the Artist

Rachel Payette

I am an artist inspired by Art Nouveau and neo-traditional styles, drawing influence from artists such as Danvillablanca, Loish, Kellogsloops, and Vitaly Morozov.

Working primarily with alcohol markers and ink, I am now expanding into the digital art space. My focus is character drawings and portraits, with a love for capturing the essence of people, characters, and mythical creatures.

Art has been my passion since childhood. Influenced deeply by instrumental music and inspired by the beauty of everyday kindness, my goal is to give form to emotions and create a visual experience that resonates with others.

Thank you for visiting my site! I hope you enjoyed exploring my art portfolio. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to connect.
